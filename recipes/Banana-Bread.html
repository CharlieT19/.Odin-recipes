Mom's Banana Bread Recipe

This is a classic recipe that my mom has made for me forever. This is THE BEST banana bread recipe you have ever seen I think this is up there with one of my favorite recipes she makes.

Ingredients

  • Preheat Oven to 350 degrees, or 325 degrees for metal pans
  • 13/4C of All Purpose Flour
  • 2/3C of Sugar
  • 2tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4tsp Salt
  • 2-3 Mashed Ripe Banana
  • 1/3C of Margarine
  • 2Tbsp of Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4C of Chestnut (Optional)

Baking Time

  1. Mix 1C of the flour w/ the other dry ingredients
  2. Add wet ingredients except for the eggs
  3. Mix w/ electric mixer for 2 min
  4. Add eggs and remaining flour mix until blended
  5. Add Nuts (Optional)
  6. Pour into bread pan, and put into oven at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes
